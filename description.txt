A lightweight tModLoader mod that:
- Draws health, mana & flight‑time bars beneath your player sprite
- Renders directional pointers to bosses and your cursor

Can partially replace features from
- Player Health and Mana Bars: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3142258166
- Cursor Indicator: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3045309304
- BossCursor: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2816694149

Key Features
🔄 Toggle on/off with a hotkey (default: P)
👹 Boss‑only mode: show pointers & bars only during boss fights
⚙️ Full ModConfig support: customize colors, scale, offsets, auto‑hide rules, hotkeys, and more

Ideal for bullet‑hell encounters—focus on your positioning without UI clutter.
